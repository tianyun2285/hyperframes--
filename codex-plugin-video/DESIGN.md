## Style Prompt

A 10-second premium tech launch reel for "Codex super plugin roundup": dark cinematic interface space, rapid UI cards flying through depth, hard chromatic cuts, scanline energy, and explosive kinetic typography. It should feel like an elite product demo compressed into an adrenaline trailer: precise, glossy, fast, and immediately actionable.

## Colors

- Background: #06070d, near-black with a cool violet bias
- Panel: #111827, glassy dark interface surfaces
- Foreground: #f4f7ff, high-contrast titanium white
- Accent primary: #39f5ff, electric cyan for code/video/browser energy
- Accent secondary: #ff3df2, magenta flash for transitions and hype
- Accent impact: #f7ff4a, laser yellow for urgent keywords

## Typography

- Display: "Archivo Black", sans-serif, heavy condensed launch voice
- Data/UI: "IBM Plex Mono", monospace, precise technical captions and labels

## Motion Rules

- Primary transitions: glitch/chromatic slices and zoom-through blocks at 0.16-0.28s.
- Card movement: fly in from depth, slight 3D rotation, then lock sharply.
- Keywords: slam in with overshoot, micro jitter, and quick scale pops.
- Ambient: scanlines, wire grids, waveform bars, orbit rings, and subtle light sweeps.

## What NOT to Do

- No soft explainer pacing.
- No pastel, beige, or generic corporate blue palette.
- No long paragraphs or instructional copy.
- No jump cuts between scenes; every scene change needs a visible transition.
- No static card grid that feels like a slide deck.
